Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

TopCheese ("we", "our", or "the app") is an NHL stats app that shows shots on goal and related information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Subscription status. When you subscribe, we use Apple and our subscription provider (RevenueCat) to know whether you have access to premium features. We do not create or store your own account; there is no sign-up or login in the app.
  • Device and app info. We may receive or use information such as your device type, operating system version, and app version so we can run and improve the app and provide support.
  • Usage data. We may collect limited, aggregated usage information (such as app interactions) to improve performance and fix issues.
  • Payments. All payments are processed by Apple. We do not see or store your payment details. Apple's payment and privacy terms apply to purchases.

How we collect it

  • Through the app. Some data (e.g. app version, subscription status) is collected automatically when you use the app.
  • Through third parties. Subscription status and related identifiers are handled by Apple and our subscription provider. If we use analytics or crash-reporting tools, they may collect usage or device data as described in their own policies.
  • When you subscribe. When you buy a subscription in the app, Apple processes the payment and our subscription provider receives information needed to grant you access (e.g. that you are a subscriber). We do not receive your card or payment details.

Why we collect it

  • To operate the app and deliver features (including premium access).
  • To process and manage subscriptions.
  • To improve performance, fix bugs, and understand how the app is used.
  • To provide support (e.g. replying to your emails).

Who we share it with

  • Apple. For payment processing and subscription management. Apple's privacy policy applies to their handling of your data.
  • Subscription provider (RevenueCat). To manage subscription status and entitlements so we can unlock premium features correctly.
  • Service providers. We may use providers for hosting and infrastructure (e.g. servers). They process data only on our behalf and only as needed to run our services.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention and deletion

We keep data only as long as needed to run the app, manage subscriptions, and meet legal obligations. If we hold data that identifies you (e.g. if you contact us by email), you can ask us to delete it by emailing support@topcheeseapp.com. Subscription and purchase history are also subject to Apple's policies and your device settings.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, or to object to certain uses. To exercise these or ask questions about your data, contact us at support@topcheeseapp.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the current version in the app or on our website. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
